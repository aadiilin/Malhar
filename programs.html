Islamic Studies

Qur'an Academy

Comprehensive Qur'anic education program offering memorization (Hifdh), proper recitation with Tajweed rules, and in-depth study of Tafseer (interpretation). Our qualified teachers follow classical and contemporary methodologies to ensure students develop a deep, meaningful connection with the Book of Allah.

Duration: Flexible levels from beginner to advanced | Ages: 5 & above

Education

Islamic Studies

A comprehensive curriculum covering Aqeedah (creed), Fiqh (jurisprudence), Hadith (prophetic traditions), Seerah (biography of the Prophet), Arabic language, and contemporary Islamic thought. Designed for students of all ages, our programs build a strong foundation in Islamic knowledge and practice.

Levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced | Format: Classroom & Online

Outreach

Community School

Supplemental education programs for children and youth, focusing on academic support, moral education, and extracurricular development. Our community school provides a nurturing environment where students receive homework assistance, character education, and enrichment activities.

Ages: 6-16 | Schedule: After-school & Weekend programs

Empowerment

Women's Empowerment

Dedicated programs for women focusing on Islamic education, skill development, entrepreneurship, and personal growth. We provide a supportive space for women to learn, connect, and develop skills that enhance their role in family and community life.

Programs: Islamic classes, vocational training, health awareness

Development

Skill Development

Vocational and technical training programs designed to equip community members with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship. Courses include computer literacy, tailoring, handicrafts, small business management, and more.

Welfare

Charity & Relief

Comprehensive social welfare initiatives including food distribution, educational sponsorships, medical assistance, and emergency relief for families in need. We believe in serving humanity as a fundamental expression of our faith and values.
