Our Story

Malharul Islam Academy was founded with a clear and noble vision: to provide accessible, quality education rooted in Islamic values while fostering comprehensive community development. Operating under the esteemed patronage of Jamia Madeenathunnoor, our academy stands as a beacon of knowledge, faith, and social service in the Vellamchira region of Thodupuzha, Idukki District, Kerala.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a vibrant educational and community center that serves hundreds of students and reaches thousands of families through our various programs and initiatives. Our journey has been one of dedication, perseverance, and unwavering commitment to the principles of Islamic education and community upliftment.

Today, Malharul Islam Academy is recognized as a trusted institution that nurtures the next generation of leaders, scholars, and community servants — individuals who are deeply rooted in their Islamic identity and equipped to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by faith and driven by purpose, we strive to make a lasting impact on individuals and communities.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic knowledge and community transformation, producing generations of faithful, knowledgeable, and service-oriented individuals who lead with integrity and compassion.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education rooted in Islamic values, foster community development through targeted initiatives, and nurture spiritual growth that translates into positive social action.

Our Values

Faith (Iman), Knowledge (Ilm), Compassion (Rahma), Excellence (Ihsan), Unity (Tawheed), Service (Khidmah) — the core principles that guide every aspect of our work.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Malharul Islam Academy.

Faith (Iman)

A strong foundation in Islamic faith that shapes character, guides actions, and provides purpose.

Knowledge (Ilm)

Commitment to acquiring and disseminating knowledge that enlightens minds and enriches souls.

Compassion (Rahma)

Extending mercy, kindness, and care to all members of the community regardless of background.

Excellence (Ihsan)

Striving for the highest standards in education, service, and all aspects of institutional life.

Unity (Tawheed)

Fostering brotherhood, cooperation, and solidarity within the community and beyond.

Service (Khidmah)

Dedication to serving humanity as an expression of faith and a pathway to divine pleasure.
