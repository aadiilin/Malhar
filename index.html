About Malharul Islam Academy

Malharul Islam Academy is a distinguished educational and community development institution operating under the esteemed umbrella of Jamia Madeenathunnoor. Located in the serene surroundings of Vellamchira, Thodupuzha, in the Idukki District of Kerala, our academy is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, Islamic values, and holistic community development.

We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are deeply rooted in their faith while being equipped to contribute meaningfully to society. Our programs span Islamic sciences, general education, vocational training, and social welfare initiatives that uplift the entire community.

  • Quality Islamic Education
  • Community Development Programs
  • Character & Leadership Building
  • Social Welfare Initiatives

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by faith and driven by purpose, we strive to make a lasting impact on individuals and communities.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic knowledge and community transformation, producing generations of faithful, knowledgeable, and service-oriented individuals who lead with integrity and compassion.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education rooted in Islamic values, foster community development through targeted initiatives, and nurture spiritual growth that translates into positive social action.

Our Values

Faith (Iman), Knowledge (Ilm), Compassion (Rahma), Excellence (Ihsan), Unity (Tawheed), Service (Khidmah) — the core principles that guide every aspect of our work.
