Annual Community Iftar Brings Together Families in Thodupuzha

June 15, 2026 Malharul Islam Academy 12 Comments
Community Iftar Event

Malharul Islam Academy hosted its annual community iftar on June 15, 2026, bringing together families from across Thodupuzha and surrounding areas for an unforgettable evening of unity, prayer, and communal harmony. The event, held at the academy's campus in Vellamchira, witnessed an overwhelming turnout of community members from diverse backgrounds.

The iftar gathering began in the late afternoon with recitation from the Holy Qur'an, followed by a collective prayer. As the Adhan (call to prayer) marked the time of Maghrib, attendees broke their fast together with dates and water before proceeding to the main meal. The atmosphere was filled with a sense of brotherhood, compassion, and shared purpose.

A Message of Unity

Speaking at the event, the academy's director emphasized the importance of community solidarity and mutual support, especially during the blessed month of Ramadan. "This iftar is more than just a meal — it is a symbol of our commitment to building bridges, fostering understanding, and strengthening the bonds that unite us as one community," he said.

"The beauty of this gathering is seeing families from all walks of life sitting together, sharing a meal, and praying side by side. This is the essence of our faith and the foundation of a strong community." — Academy Director

Community Engagement

The iftar also featured a special lecture on community harmony and the Islamic principles of social responsibility. Children from the academy's Qur'an program performed a beautiful nasheed (Islamic song) that was met with heartfelt applause. The event concluded with Isha and Taraweeh prayers, leaving attendees spiritually uplifted and deeply connected.

Volunteers from the academy's community development program worked tirelessly to organize the event, ensuring that everything from the meal preparation to the seating arrangements reflected the spirit of hospitality that Islam encourages. Local businesses and community members also contributed generously to make the event a success.

Looking Ahead

Malharul Islam Academy plans to make the community iftar an annual tradition, with hopes of expanding it to include even more families in the coming years. The academy continues to serve as a beacon of light and unity in the Thodupuzha region, bringing people together through faith, education, and community service.

Stay connected with Malharul Islam Academy for more updates on upcoming events, programs, and community initiatives. Together, we build a stronger, more compassionate community.
